Claim your restaurant

Your restaurant is probably already on Flavorites. Make it yours.

Diners may already be saving your restaurant as a Flavorite, browsing your hours, and recommending you to friends. Claiming your page is free, takes minutes, and puts you in control of how your restaurant shows up.

Get Flavorites Business for iOS

Claiming happens in the Flavorites Business app. Free on the App Store.

How claiming works

  1. Find your restaurant

    Download Flavorites Business, create your owner account with Apple, Google, or email, and search for your restaurant. Most restaurants already have a page built from public listing data.

  2. Verify you own it

    Confirm ownership with your business email. Verification protects your page — only a verified owner can publish changes or run campaigns for the restaurant.

  3. Not listed yet? Create your page

    If your restaurant isn't on Flavorites, create the page in the app. New pages are reviewed by the Flavorites team — typically within 24–48 hours — before going live for diners.

  4. Publish and grow

    Once approved, your edits go live on the same page diners see: story, hours, photos, menu highlights. From there you can invite staff, launch a referral campaign, and track results.

What claiming unlocks

  • Your storefront, your voice — correct your hours, tell your story, showcase your best dishes
  • Supporter signals — see how many diners have Flavorited and are following your restaurant
  • Referral campaigns — reward fans for bringing you verified customers (how the program works)
  • Private drops — capped releases and private menus for your biggest supporters
  • Staff roles — give employees QR verification access without handing over the keys

Is my restaurant eligible?

If you own or manage a restaurant, café, bar, bakery, food truck, or counter — yes. You'll need a business email for verification and an iPhone for the Flavorites Business app. Claiming is free, and nothing obligates you to run paid campaigns.

Take control of your page today.

Free to claim, free to manage. Campaigns are optional and prepaid — you decide if and when to spend.
