N° 01Why Flavorites

Pay for verified visits, not clicks.

Traditional restaurant advertising charges you when someone taps an ad — whether or not they ever show up. Flavorites flips the model: your budget moves only when a real customer is standing in your restaurant and your team confirms it.

N° 02How it works

From claim to first verified customer.

  1. Claim your restaurant

    Verify ownership with your business email and take control of your Flavorites page. New restaurant pages are reviewed within 24–48 hours.

  2. Publish your storefront

    Your hours, story, menu highlights, and photos update the same page hungry customers browse in the Flavorites app.

  3. Fund a referral campaign

    Set a fixed bounty per verified visit and prepay a campaign wallet. You choose who can refer and who qualifies — all customers or first-timers only.

  4. Scan, verify, done

    Customers arrive with a QR visit pass. Your staff scans it, enters the order subtotal, and the reward is released to the referrer after a 72-hour dispute window.

N° 03Built-in safeguards

Every cent, accounted for.

Your campaign wallet works like a till at closing time: every top-up, every reserved bounty, every reversal is a balanced double-entry ledger transaction you can audit in the app.

  • A.
    Prepaid and capped by design.

    A campaign can never spend more than its wallet, and it pauses itself when the balance can't cover the next bounty.

  • B.
    72 hours to dispute anything.

    Every reward stays pending after verification. If a redemption looks wrong, dispute it before a cent moves.

  • C.
    Fraud controls are standard.

    One-time QR passes, no self-referrals, no staff rewards at their own restaurant, one reward per table.

N° 05Common questions

Restaurant owners ask us…

How much does Flavorites Business cost?

Claiming and managing your storefront is free. Referral campaigns are prepaid performance marketing: you fund a campaign wallet (minimum $100) and set a fixed bounty per verified visit (minimum $4). There's no subscription and no per-click charge. See pricing details.

Do I need to change my POS or checkout?

No. Verification is a QR scan in the Flavorites Business app plus a typed order subtotal. There's no POS integration, no menu discount, and nothing changes about how customers pay.

Who can refer customers to my restaurant?

Only Flavorites users who have saved your restaurant as one of their Flavorites — genuine fans. You can additionally require a minimum follower count or restrict referrals to a private list you invite.
